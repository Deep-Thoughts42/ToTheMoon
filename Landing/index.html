WELCOME TO COLEXO

Build a planet together.

In this simulation, you get to work with the rest of the internet to “build” a planet suitable for human habitation. Together, you must strike the right balance between water content, flora, albedo, and oxygen levels.

As the planet progresses, it will evolve, changing its Habitability Meter and population limit. Once the meter reaches 100%, we will be able to launch a rocket and colonize it.
